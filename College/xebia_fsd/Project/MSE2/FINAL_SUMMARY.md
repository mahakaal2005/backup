### **Problem (1 min):**

### **Solution (2 min):**
"The app has 3 components: a form to write messages, cards to display them, and logic to check time. Messages are stored in the browser and auto-unlock when the time arrives."

### **Technical (2 min):**
"I used React for components, HTML/CSS for UI, and localStorage for data. The key part is the time comparison - every 30 seconds the app checks if current time >= unlock time."
